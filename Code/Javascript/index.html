Location Availability System

Introduction:
Considering that UCSD is already a large campus with more and more students beingenrolled every year, it is no wonder that students are consistently finding major study/diningspots such as the library and price center crowded. This situation is difficult to avoid since it ishard to tell whether a location is crowded or not in real-time unless the student is present at thelocation. In order to address this issue, our team decided to create a product that can help achievethis function in order to improve the day to day lives of students at UCSD.Our product is a real-time population tracker that determines the number of students at alocation through the use of sensors. The product consists of a Raspberry Pi which serves as acontrol-center and ultrasonic sensors that are logically programmed to determine the number ofpeople going into and coming out of a designated location at a certain time.
